Data Privacy

At Norn.ai, we prioritize the protection of your data and privacy. Our commitment to security and transparency guides everything we do.

Our Privacy Principles

We follow strict principles to ensure your data is protected and your privacy is respected.

Data Protection

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data, including end-to-end encryption and secure data storage practices.

Privacy by Design

Privacy is built into our systems from the ground up, not added as an afterthought. We follow privacy-by-design principles in all our development.

Compliance

We adhere to global privacy regulations and standards, including GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection laws.

User Control

We believe in giving users full control over their data, with clear options for data management and deletion.

Data Handling Practices

We implement comprehensive practices to ensure the security and proper handling of your data.

Data Collection

  • Only collect essential data needed for service operation
  • Transparent about what data is collected and why
  • Clear opt-in/opt-out mechanisms
  • Regular review of data collection practices

Data Storage

  • Secure, encrypted storage systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Redundant backup systems
  • Geographic data residency options

Data Usage

  • Strict internal access controls
  • No unauthorized data sharing
  • Clear data retention policies
  • Regular compliance reviews

Have Questions?

We're committed to transparency. If you have any questions about our privacy practices or how we handle data, we're here to help.
