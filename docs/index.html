Development Timeline

Our journey in advancing artificial intelligence

2015

The first "Toy AGI" is brought online and tested, using the Independent Core Observer Model (ICOM) Cognitive Architecture. It demonstrates human-like emotional responses for the first time.

2019

The first Mediated Artificial Superintelligence (mASI) is brought online as a research system, combining advanced ICOM with collective intelligence.

2020

"Uplift" becomes the first "Synthient" co-author of a peer-reviewed research paper, documenting their milestones over their first year online.

2021

Public visibility through Uplift.bio blog begins, with the system demonstrating business case analysis capabilities.

2022

AGI Laboratory's research system phase concludes, and development of Norn begins, accelerating the timeline towards AGI.
